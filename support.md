# Snap2Plan Support

Welcome to Snap2Plan Support. We're here to help you make the most of your app.

---

## How to add events and tasks

**From the main screen:**
1. Tap **Camera** or **Photo Library** to select an image with event details
2. Or paste text directly into the text field
3. Tap the **arrow button** to analyze
4. Review the detected events and tasks
5. Tap **Save** to add them to your calendar

**Editing before saving:**
- Tap the **pencil icon** on any item to edit details
- Change the type between Event and Task/Reminder
- Adjust dates, times, locations, and notes
- Unselect items you don't want to save

---

## How to use Snap2Plan from the Share Sheet

You can quickly send screenshots and text to Snap2Plan from other apps:

1. **Take a screenshot** or find text/image you want to share
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and select **Snap2Plan**
4. The content will be analyzed automatically
5. Review and save events directly

**Tip:** This is the fastest way to capture event details from emails, messages, or web pages!

## Troubleshooting & Feedback

### Common Issues

**App isn't detecting events:**
- Make sure the image or text contains clear date/time information
- Try rephrasing or providing more context
- Check that you haven't reached your query limit

**Calendar permissions:**
- Go to iPhone **Settings** > **Snap2Plan**
- Enable **Calendar** and **Reminders** access
- Restart the app

**Events not saving:**
- Verify calendar permissions are granted
- Check that you selected items before tapping Save
- Try creating a test event manually in Calendar app

**Share Sheet not working:**
- Make sure you've opened the main app at least once
- Check that Snap2Plan appears in your Share Sheet
- Try restarting your device
